Our Vision

A non-judgmental space to deepen your practice.

"Our vision is to create a space for young people in Los Angeles to care for their inner-wellbeing through yoga, soundbaths, empowering workshops & good food."

We are Vraja & Kunja, the owners of Studio Om. Yoga is grounded in the timeless science of Bhakti—wisdom for genuine self-growth, happiness, and connection to the love supreme.
